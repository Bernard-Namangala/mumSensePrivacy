🚨 The Problem

Grocery prices are rising globally, but household budgets aren't keeping up. The average family overspends by 15% or more on their weekly shop, not because they're careless, but because they simply don't have time to compare prices, track spending patterns, or research cheaper alternatives.

For busy mums, this problem compounds. They're the primary household shoppers, yet they have the least amount of spare time to optimise their spending. And when it comes to building long-term financial security, most investing apps feel intimidating, jargon-heavy, and designed for people who already have money.

The result: families leak money on groceries every week, savings stay flat, and financial confidence remains out of reach.

👩‍👧‍👦 Target Audience

MumSense is built for Rebecca Louise's audience: busy mums aged 25-45 who want practical, fast, and actionable financial guidance, not theory. They are:

  • Time-poor - juggling work, kids, and household responsibilities with minutes, not hours, to spare
  • Budget-conscious - actively looking for ways to reduce everyday costs like groceries, home essentials, and utilities
  • Financially curious but underserved - interested in saving and investing but put off by apps that assume prior knowledge or disposable income
  • Community-driven - trust peer recommendations from other mums over generic advertising

MumSense meets them where they already are: in the supermarket aisle, at the kitchen table reviewing receipts, or during a 10-minute coffee break.

💡 The Solution

MumSense is a mobile app that helps busy mums save money on everyday shopping and learn to grow what they save, powered by AI that gets smarter with every receipt scanned.

In-Store Price Check
Scan or type any product while shopping. See cheaper options at nearby stores instantly.
AI Receipt Scanning
Snap a photo of any receipt. AI extracts every item, store, and total automatically.
Smart Insights
Spending breakdowns by category and store, with personalised "Swap & Save" suggestions.
AI Budget Coach
Conversational AI that analyses your receipts and builds custom savings plans.
Community Hub
Browse trolleys, discover local deals, and join bulk buys shared by other mums.
Learn to Invest
AI-generated daily lessons, a practice portfolio, and a personal investment coach.

Growth levers:

  • Community network effect - shared trolleys, deals, and bulk buys make the app more valuable as more mums join
  • Receipt-driven lock-in - the more receipts scanned, the smarter insights become, increasing switching cost
  • Organic sharing - trolleys and deals are inherently shareable, driving word-of-mouth acquisition
  • Investing upsell - the Grow module targets users who've already saved money and want to do more with it

🏆 Why MumSense Wins

Most budgeting apps track where money went. MumSense tells you where money is going next and how to keep more of it. The combination of real-time price checking, AI-powered receipt analysis, community-driven savings, and accessible investing education in a single app is unique in the market.

Every feature is designed around one question: "Can a busy mum use this in under 60 seconds?" If the answer is no, we simplify it until it is.
