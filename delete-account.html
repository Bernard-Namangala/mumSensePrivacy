Delete Your MumSense Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request full deletion of your MumSense account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MumSense app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom-right icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Alternatively, email us at the address below and we will process your request within 48 hours.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All scanned receipts and purchase history
  • AI insights and budget data
  • Community posts, trolleys, and bulk buy participation
  • Investment profile and simulated portfolio
  • Notification preferences and push tokens

What May Be Retained

  • Anonymized, aggregated price data contributed to community pricing (not linked to your identity)
  • Data required by law for up to 90 days

Timeframe

Account deletion is processed immediately in-app. If requested via email, deletion is completed within 48 hours.
